Please record your house league match results carefully on the score sheets using one of the following two methods.

Method 1 (Most Popular)

Write the number of games you won on your row under your opponent's column. Write the number of games your opponent won on their row under your column. For example, if Heather Wallace beat Jansher Kahn 3 games to 1, record the score as follows:
